Happiness

The visualisations below show how multiple factors such as the economy, health and trust of the government affects the happiness of the people.

Happiness Around The World

The figure on the right represents the happiness index in every country. Red represents that the happiness index is VERY LOW, Yellow represents that the happiness index is MODERATE and GREEN represents that the happiness index is High

How Happiness Affects Health

The scatterplot aims to provide evidence that happiness and health co-relates with each other. We can see an increase in health (life expectency) of a country when there is an increase in the happiness index of the country. Thus, the state of our health DOES affect our happiness as humans.

Economy and Happiness

The economy of a country plays a big role in a country as it determines whether a country is prosperous or not. According to the line chart on the right, the economy(GDP) of a country DOES affect a country as there is an increase in happiness index when a country has a higher GDP.
